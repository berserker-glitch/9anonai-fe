# Navigating Legal Matters in Morocco from Abroad

As a Moroccan Residing Abroad (MRE), managing legal affairs in Morocco can be challenging. Whether it's property management, family status, or investment, staying informed is key.

## Real Estate and Property Rights
Buying or selling property in Morocco involves specific procedures. Ensure all transactions are notarized. For MREs, there are often tax incentives for property investment, but be aware of the new regulations regarding non-resident taxation introduced in 2026.

## Family Law
Marriage, divorce, and inheritance matters are governed by the Moudawana. It's crucial to register any civil status changes made abroad with the Moroccan consulate to ensure they are recognized in Morocco.

## Investment Incentives
Morocco offers various incentives for MREs looking to invest, including tax breaks and simplified administrative procedures through the Regional Investment Centers (CRI).
