# Health for All: A Dream Coming True

AMO for all is the largest social project in modern Morocco. In 2026, the system covers over 22 million additional beneficiaries (AMO Tadamon).

## Expanding Care Basket
This year sees the integration of new pathologies and expensive drugs, relieving families, especially for cancers and chronic diseases.

## Financing Challenge
To last, the system requires regular adherence from professionals and freelancers. The State bets on digitalization to encourage spontaneous payment and achieve real universal health coverage.
