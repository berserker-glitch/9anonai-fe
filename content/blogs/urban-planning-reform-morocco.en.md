# Urban Planning: New Rules of the Game

After 30 years, Law 25.90 needed an update to keep up with rapid urbanization.

## Tacit Permits
Major novelty: "silence means consent" for certain administrative authorizations, to free investment from bureaucracy.

## Smart Specifications
Adoption of flexible rules depending on the zone (tourist, industrial), with stricter sanctions against violations disfiguring the urban landscape.
