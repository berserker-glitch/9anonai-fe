# Renaissance of the Moroccan Fleet

Morocco suffers from the weakness of its fleet, depending on foreign companies.

## Maritime Leasing
Reduction of customs duties and taxes on ship leasing, encouraging Moroccan investors to acquire or lease ships at competitive costs.

## Strategic Objective
Regain "maritime sovereignty" and reduce logistics costs weighing on exports.
