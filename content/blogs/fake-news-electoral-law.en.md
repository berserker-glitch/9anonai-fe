# Fighting Electoral Disinformation

In the age of "Deepfakes", manipulating elections through rumors threatens democracy. Morocco integrates strict sanctions into electoral law.

## What the Law Says
Punishable by prison and fine is anyone who spreads, in bad faith, false news or slanderous denunciations influencing the vote or harming candidates' reputation.

## Liberty vs Responsibility
Rights defenders fear abusive use to silence criticism. The legislator assures the goal is to "protect the sincerity of the ballot" and not prevent factual political criticism.
